Mi Sangre


My interests are very diverse. I enjoy iOS and web development, but I also love to draw whenever I get the chance. In high school, I was in this Graphic Design & Illustration Department, where I learned all about the fundamentals of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. In my spare time, I love to garden, play tennis with my friends, and play very old video games on my NintendoDS.


My parents are both from Tempoal,Veracruz which is on the east coast of Mexico. Playing in the background is one of the biggest events in our culture, Xantolo, where we commemorate the memories of our deceased relatives. Mexican culture has definitely impacted everything I do. I love cooking authentic mexican dishes and I love to listen to the traditional music of my people.
