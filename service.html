TAMU UNICEF

Vice President 2019-21


This will be my third year being a part of TAMU UNICEF and the experience has been life-changing. I've had the priveledge of being able to empower my community to help the children of the world, and our cause is something that's very near and dear to my heart. It's very disheartening to see children all around the world that are forced to grow up to deal with adult problems. So, as a member of TAMU UNICEF, I proudly devote my time with one purpose in mind: to let kids be kids. From selling baked goods, to volunteering at the Food Bank, to tutoring kids at Neal Recreational Center, every step counts in working towards the survival, protection, and development of children worldwide.
